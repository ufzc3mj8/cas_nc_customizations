Students with Invalid Tracks in ~[if.district.office]All Schools[else]~[schoolname][/if.district.office]

This report selects any students whose Track field is populated with something other than A,B,C,D,E, or F.

Selecting a record will take you to the students Transfer Info page, where the invalid enrollments track may be corrected by selecting the Entry Date, then modifying the Track to be either blank or the students actual school track.
